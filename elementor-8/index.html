ABOUT ME

Join me, not in a tapestry, but in a heartfelt dialogue
where design is a personal expression, a bridge between
cultures, and a celebration of diversity.

Picture the resilient Zungueira, balancing baskets on her head, traversing kilometers to support her kin. My journey echoes that spirit, with my basket carrying not goods, but an overflow of creativity, ideas, and a diversity of concepts. Over 15 years in the realm of graphic design, branding, and advertising have been a journey of infusing tradition into the fabric of innovation. My path in visual communication has been an exploration of marrying heritage with contemporary concepts, creating a harmonious blend that resonates through every visual storytelling expression.

My academic pursuits led me to a Master of Science in Social Sciences, Spatial Designs, and Society – a lens through which I analyze the symbiotic relationship between design and our environment. Having lived in different corners of the world and collaborated with minds of varied cultures, I've become a shapeshifter, effortlessly adapting to the colors of different contexts.
But who am I beyond the professional veil? I'm a creative nomad, driven by an intrinsic desire to infuse my roots into every stroke of design. It's a personal mission, a commitment to preserve the essence of my heritage in a world that sometimes forgets to look back.

In my journey, I've become a storyteller – not of commercial campaigns, but of authentic narratives that resonate with the human experience. I've honed my skills in visual storytelling, concept design, and senior graphic design, not just as tools for a trade, but as brushes to paint the emotions and stories that lie beneath the surface.

As a communication professional, I don't just bring skills; I bring a piece of me – quick adaptability to diverse needs, a strategic mindset, and a proactive, helpful attitude. It's not about campaigns; it's about understanding and connecting with people. It's not about brand management; it's about weaving identity into every project.
